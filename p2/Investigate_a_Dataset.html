Exploratory Data Analysis¶

our first question is knowing which period (60s, 70s...) has the highest rating (vote_average)

Research Question 1 (which period has the highest rating)¶

the oldest movie in the data set was in 1960 and the most recent one is in 2015, so we have 6 periods.

we will use the cut function to divide the years.

In [60]:
bin_edges=[1959,1969,1979,1989,1999,2009,2015]
bin_names=['60s','70s','80s','90s','2000-2009','2010-2015']
df['period']=pd.cut(df['release_year'],bin_edges,labels=bin_names)

Conclusions¶

after we saw the plots, we found that the 70s period has the highest average rating which is equal to 6.36 . this result might be inaccurate because the number of older movies is less than the recent ones in this database.

we have also found that there is no correlation between the budget of a movie and its rating, it is also the same for the popularity of a movie and its rating. higher popularity and budget were not associated with higher movie rating.
